Mini Backpack

Amazon Review • Everyday Use

A compact everyday backpack with just the right amount of space for essentials without feeling bulky.

I was looking for a small backpack for everyday use, and this one ended up being exactly what I needed.

It has two main compartments that are spacious enough to carry things like a water bottle, wallet, keys, and other daily essentials without feeling cramped. There’s also a smaller compartment that works well for quick-access items.

One feature I really like is the hidden zipper pocket inside, which is perfect for storing money or anything you want to keep a little more secure. The small decorative details on the zipper are also a nice touch—they add some personality without being too much.

Size-wise, it strikes a good balance. It’s big enough to be useful and noticeable, but not so large that it feels bulky or gets in the way.

I usually stick to black bags, but I wanted something different this time, and this one immediately caught my eye. I’m really happy with it and would definitely recommend it.

Frozen Dinnerware Set (5-Piece)

Amazon Purchase • Kids

A complete kids dinnerware set that’s fun, practical, and perfect for Frozen fans.

I purchased this Frozen dinnerware set for my niece since she loves the movie, and it ended up being a really great choice.

The biggest plus is that it comes as a full set, so I didn’t have to piece anything together. It includes a plate, bowl, utensils, and a water bottle, which made it super convenient.

The water bottle was one of the standout items. It didn’t leak or spill, even when it was left laying on the floor, which is a big deal for kids.

The spoon and fork were perfectly sized for a child, making it easy for her to use on her own. The entire set also held up really well over time and didn’t feel cheap.

I’m not completely sure if it’s microwave safe, but I did use it in the microwave a few times without any issues.

Overall, this was a great buy. It’s practical, durable, and perfect if you’re shopping for a kid who loves Frozen.

Portable Neck Fan

Amazon Purchase • Outdoor Use

A hands-free fan that works, but doesn’t quite deliver the cooling effect you might expect.

This neck fan was an okay purchase overall. It works, but it didn’t fully meet my expectations.

The fan does blow air and can be helpful when you’re outside in the heat, but it’s important to know that it’s just circulating the air around you. So if it’s hot outside, you’re still getting hot air.

I did like that the fans are adjustable, which makes it easy to direct airflow where you want it. It’s also very easy to charge and use.

I only ended up using it a few times because it wasn’t really for me. I think it would be better if it actually produced cooler or colder air instead of just moving warm air around.

Overall, it’s not a bad product—it just depends on what you’re expecting from it.

Personal purchase.

Extra Deep Pocket Sheet Set

Amazon Purchase • Bedding

Soft, well-fitting sheets that are perfect for thicker mattresses and great for the price.

These sheets were a great purchase, especially if you have a thicker mattress.

The fitted sheet fits perfectly without slipping off, which is something I’ve had issues with in the past. The extra deep pockets make a big difference.

The texture is one of the best parts—they’re soft with a smooth, almost silky feel that makes them really comfortable to sleep on.

I also liked the color options. There’s a good variety to choose from, and the one I picked looked just as good in person.

The set comes with everything you need: a fitted sheet, flat sheet, and two pillowcases, which makes it a great value.

Overall, I would definitely buy these again and would recommend them if you’re looking for comfortable sheets that actually fit properly.
